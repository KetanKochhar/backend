Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how CustomWear ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you visit our website customwear.co.in.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following personal information when you register or place an order on our website:

  • Full Name
  • Email Address
  • Phone Number
  • Date of Birth
  • Shipping Address

2. Payment Information

We use Razorpay as our third-party payment gateway provider. We do not store your payment details on our servers.

3. Sharing of Information

We do not sell or rent your personal data. However, we share your pincode with the Indian Post service solely for shipping purposes.

4. Data Storage

All user data is securely stored in cloud storage. Our business is located in Rajkot, India.

5. User Accounts

Users have the option to create an account for a personalized experience and to manage their orders.

6. Marketing

We may use your email address to send promotional offers, updates, or newsletters. You can opt out at any time by clicking the unsubscribe link in our emails.

7. Cookies and Tracking

We do not currently use cookies or third-party tracking tools.
